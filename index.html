Who We Are

A Legacy of Engineering Excellence in Pakistan

Concraft Pakistan has been delivering landmark construction projects since 1992. From sprawling industrial complexes to iconic commercial buildings, our portfolio reflects a commitment to precision, safety, and enduring quality that our clients trust.

Operating from four regional offices across Pakistan, we provide end-to-end services from feasibility and design through to execution and handover.

  • In-house structural, civil and MEP engineering teams
  • On-time delivery record across 120+ completed projects
  • Transparent project management with regular client reporting
  • Full compliance with Pakistan Building Control Authority standards
  • Dedicated HSE team on every active construction site

Our Journey

Milestones & History

Three decades of engineering excellence, project by project.

1992

Company Founded

Concraft Pakistan established in Karachi with a mission to deliver quality civil and construction engineering projects across the country.

1998

PEC Registration

Achieved Pakistan Engineering Council registration at Reg. No. 2409, cementing our commitment to professional and regulated engineering practice.

2005

Cavish Court Delivered

Completed Cavish Court, one of our landmark institutional projects, establishing Concraft as a trusted name in large-scale Karachi construction.

2010

Karachi Tools, Dies & Moulds Centre

Delivered this prestigious SMEDA-backed industrial facility, expanding our portfolio into government and development-sector projects.

2014–2015

TCS Campus — Dual Phases

Completed both phases of the TCS Express and Logistics Centre, forging a long-term relationship with one of Pakistan's leading logistics companies.

2022

NNG Gymkhana — Groundbreaking

Foundation stone laid for the prestigious NNG Gymkhana project, marking Concraft's entry into landmark community construction.
